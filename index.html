Reconnect with Nature, Culture and Comfort

Hosted by Local Families

Guests are warmly welcomed by island families, indulging in authentic hospitality and embracing the essence of traditional kampung life.

Charming Village Residences

Experience accommodations within quaint, family homes that exude the timeless charm of traditional village architecture.

Immersive Cultural Journey

Engage in enriching daily rituals, from savoring local cuisine to participating in heritage crafts and farming traditions.

Authentic Cultural Heritage

Guests are immersed in the rich tapestry of customs, traditions, and timeless values that define the local community.




Empowering Community Connections

The homestay experience is rooted in community-led initiatives, fostering sustainable development and enhancing local livelihoods.

Rustic Simplicity

Embrace the elegance of modest, yet comfortable amenities that echo the unadorned beauty of kampung life.

Nature’s Splendor

Revel in Pulau Tuba’s pristine landscapes, with untouched beaches, lush forests, and vibrant wildlife becoming an integral part of your stay.

Our Homestays

Indulge in an unforgettable Kampung Homestay adventure on Pulau Tuba! Hosted by the warm-hearted islanders of Dayang Tuba Lestari, guests are treated to genuine kampung hospitality. Immerse yourself fully in the vibrant culture and traditions, staying with local families in their charming village homes—experience the true essence of Pulau Tuba like never before!
